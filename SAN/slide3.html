• A fabric is just the hardware that glues everything together
  • NICS, cabling, fibre switches, storage devices
  • A brocade, for example, is a brand of fibre switch
  • dual-fabric means two separate networks
  • The SAN fabric is used ONLY for SAN, not for other traffic
  • SAN NICs are called HBAs: Host Bus Adapters
  • QLogic, Emulex, JNI all make HBAs
