• T/DS lines:
    DS0: 64 kbps
    T1/DS1: 24 DS0's, 1.544 Mbps
    E1: 30 64kbps channels, 2.048 Mbps (Euro)
    • T3/DS3: 672 DS0's, 44.736 Mbps
    T4/DS4: 4032 DS0's, 274.176 Mbps
  • T/DS devices are asynchronous, with independent clocks in the devices
  • Trunk3/Trunk4 must be run over fiber or microwave
  • OC (Optical Carrier)/SONET lines:
    OC-1: 51.48 Mbps
    OC-3: 155.52 Mbps
    OC-12: 622.08 Mbps
    OC-48: 2.4 Gbps
    OC-192: 9.6 Gbps
  • SONET devices are synch'd to an atomic clock: better throughput
