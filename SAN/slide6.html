• All devices can see all other devices: BAD
  • zoning: allows grouping of nodes by ports, names or addresses: VLANing
  • zoning is inclusion based, allows any-to-any connectivity
  • hard zoning: zoning by ports
  • one machine cannot be in two hard zones
  • soft zoning follows device names
  • soft zoning is implemented through software, and can be circumvented
